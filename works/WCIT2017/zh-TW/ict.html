Category：Intelligent Transportation
Project：Bick Sharing System
Company：Mciroprogram Information LtD.
Website：http://www.program.com.tw/
Project Features：
YouBike- a public bike sharing system in Taipei is launched in 2009 by Taipei City Government. There are over 6,000 bicycles and 190 stations,250 million times in Taipei, and over 70,000 people ride bicycle every day. Microprogram is responsible for RFIＤidentify system, micropayment, multi-payment, member system, stations and bikes management system. Citizen can register as a Youbike member with an EasyCard and cellphone, Just Beep, hire bikes from one station and return them to any other.
Project Summary：
1.Hire bikes from one station and return them to any other. Multi-Payment and Self-Service
Why YouBike is so popular in Taiwan？The biggest reason is that people can easily rent on their own, rent and return in every station. This convenient causes a little yellow trend. Citizen can register as a member with an EasyCard, Cellphone number through official website, mobile app and KIOSK. Then use a registered card on the sensor pad on the dick to confirm your rental., pull the bike backward after 3 seconds. When returning, push bike into the dock and put your card on the sensor again, system compute the rate fee and pay it with card. Youbike also provide mutil-payment for foreigner and people who do not have EasyCard, let unregistered people can rent bikes with credit card, mobile micro payment. The system-wide adoption KIOKS self-service, with 24 hours customer service line, providing year-roundrental services.

2.Multi-interface, easy to use
YouBike provide mitul-interface, such as smart phone app, official website, KIOSK, so that people can get latest news, register member, real-time vehicle and parking queries. Single rental user can rent bike with mobile micro payment, credit card and other ways to rent bike through KIOSK. YouBike official APP built in smart phone GPS function, add on map API, so that user can search nearest station, vehicle / parking situation, destination navigation, check history ride record, vehicle maintenance notification and other convenience features.

3.Back-end management system, remote monitoring and automatic management
Complete remote control management software, so that mangers can manage vehicle / parking situation, monitor status of internal components and equipments, member accounts, general member and group member ,rent fees, station status and maintenance scheduling.
Category：Smart Healthcare
Project：Enabling All-round Intelligent Healthcare Services- Far Eastern Memorial Hospital Provide Optimized Medical Environment
Company：Advantech Co., Ltd.
Website：http://hos.femh.org.tw/newfemh/NetFemhEn/index_about_xml.aspx?sub=5
Project Features：
Far Eastern Memorial Hospital continues to improve its professional medical practice, and at the same time, continue to construct a total healthcare service system for patient-centered ICT applications. In 2014, Far Eastern Memorial Hospital implemented a total digital solution with various features like a data cloud, intelligent queuing and broadcasting, and system and video surveillance in its newly extended building, allowing the hospital to provide advanced outpatient services and ward room support to increase patients’ satisfaction in the medical environment. This so-called iHospital solution provided three systems: Intelligent calling & queuing system, multimedia signage system, and an interactive TV system for ward rooms. The Intelligent calling & queuing system, and multimedia signage system, delivers treatment progress, healthcare videos and bulletin board information in public spaces, clinical areas, on counters and in the pharmacy. The interactive TV system offers various services in the ward room like entertainment programs, hospital information, and medication knowledge.
Project Summary：
In hospital outpatient departments across Taiwan, it is a common sight these days to see elderly people, some with multiple chronic diseases, shuttling between clinics to receive treatment. This not only makes the patients feel exhausted, but it's also frustrating for the families who accompany them. This kind of time-consuming multi-clinic transfer process is clearly unsatisfactory for most patients.

Far Eastern Memorial Hospital continues to improve its professional medical practice, and at the same time, continue to construct a total healthcare service system for patient-centered ICT applications. In 2014, Far Eastern Memorial Hospital implemented a total digital solution with various features like a data cloud, intelligent queuing and broadcasting, and system and video surveillance in its newly extended building, allowing the hospital to provide advanced outpatient services and ward room support to increase patients’ satisfaction in the medical environment. This so-called iHospital solution provided three systems: Intelligent calling & queuing system, multimedia signage system, and an interactive TV system for ward rooms. The Intelligent calling & queuing system, and multimedia signage system, delivers treatment progress, healthcare videos and bulletin board information in public spaces, clinical areas, on counters and in the pharmacy. The interactive TV system offers various services in the ward room like entertainment programs, hospital information, and medication knowledge.

This new iHospital total solution provides a multitude of benefits to patients. For example, from the interactive touch kiosk in the hospital lobby, users can register for multiple clinics at the same time with one touch. And to avoid patient waiting times, the hospital now provides a convenient phone App to check on a clinic's progress on their mobile device. For inpatients, the 42 inch LED display and multimedia PC box offers a variety of healthcare and medication data, so patients no longer need to bring their own electronic devices to the hospital to get connected. If a particular patient is under operation, their family can even receive progress via the TV system in the ward room rather than waiting outside the operating room.

YouBike provide mitul-interface, such as smart phone app, official website, KIOSK, so that people can get latest news, register member, real-time vehicle and parking queries. Single rental user can rent bike with mobile micro payment, credit card and other ways to rent bike through KIOSK. YouBike official APP built in smart phone GPS function, add on map API, so that user can search nearest station, vehicle / parking situation, destination navigation, check history ride record, vehicle maintenance notification and other convenience features.

The new upcoming iHospital building facility of the Far Eastern Memorial Hospital not only creates a high-quality healthcare environment for doctors, nurses and patients, it also potentially improves the working efficiency of all hospitals.
Category：Smart Education
Project：ASUS Smart Education Cloud Solution
Company：ASUS Cloud Corporation
Website：http://www.asuscloud.com/
Project Features：
1.The only cloud platform in Taiwan that is endorsed by the central government, local governments, and universities provides citywide service to tens of thousands of users, featuring an open API architecture to enable interaction between applications and data and speed up education ecosystem development.

2.Professional pedagogies are integrated to develop very rich education applications such as flipped teaching, mobile learning, programming education, simultaneous sharing, and an education apps market.

3.Independent server, software, and endpoint device technologies, which have also been exported to Chongqing.
Project Summary：
The ASUS smart education cloud solution integrates a cloud service platform, education apps, and an education apps market, leveraging new education services and technologies to enable education transformation and deliver the greatest value of education to suit different aptitudes and different levels of talents of students.

【Cloud service platform】
The cloud service platform – with big data and distributed computing features -- is accessible to numerous users and heterogeneous devices. It can have different apps and content markets integrated to provide all the data and resources that education requires. Via its open API, the platform is compatible with different platforms as well as various apps and data to have lifelong learning records accumulated on the same platform. Moreover, its big data for aptitude analysis helps produce useful education information as well as remedial applications to suit different aptitudes and different levels of talent of students.

【Education application】
Cloud storage space：It can store and manage personal lifelong learning records, including teaching materials, class handouts, and relevant digital contents. It also supports cross-platform, multiple-device synchronization to enable data sharing anytime and anywhere, realize mobile learning, and facilitate knowledge exchange.
ASUS Monkeys learning management system: It factors in teaching professionalism and related know-how to provide complete learning and learning management services. Its QR code-based interactive teaching streamlines the interaction between students and teachers, while its modularization of teaching facilitates interaction between the platform and learning before, during, and after class. Moreover, different analysis results integrated into relevant teaching contents help suit different aptitudes and different levels of talent of students.
EZoApp programming education tool: It is an HTML5 mobile app ”crash” teaching tool that encourages creative thinking among students and teachers. By simply drag and pull, a cloud app can be developed in only five minutes to generate highly valuable contents and strengthen innovative learning.

【Education apps market】
The education apps market is a pool of education resources provided by the public and private sector and independently developed by students and teachers. Such resources can be developed into a variety of applications, teaching materials, and class handouts that are readily available before, during, and after class.

The smart education cloud is based on the independent endpoint device, software, and server technologies of ASUS. Such ASUS technologies are highly flexible and have also been successfully exported to Chongqing to develop an education cloud platform specifically for 5.5 million students and teachers there. The platform includes an apps and digital contents market to help realize smart education in Chongqing.
